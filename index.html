WEEKLY MILESTONES

Your week,
already written

PULSE pulls your live MSX milestones and the week's real activity — meetings, emails, follow-ups — and drafts the weekly progress narrative for each one. You review, tweak, and save. It files everything by fiscal week automatically.

  • AI-drafted weekly progress from your actual M365 activity
  • Direct / Partner / Internal evidence classification
  • One-click push of the update back to CRM (forecast comments)
  • Task-gap and commitment flags so nothing slips
SE EXECUTION TRACKER

Every motion,
scored

A live scorecard across eight strategic SE motions — SQL / Database Gravity, Fabric Attach, Compete-to-Win, Developer + Agentic, Sovereignty, Security Attach, HoK PoC and CAF. See attach coverage and the ACR sitting in the gap, scoped to your discipline via RAIN Qualifier 2.

  • Coverage % and gap ACR per motion
  • Discipline-aware: primary vs adjacent / hand-off
  • Territory whitespace with fit-scored AI attach plays
  • A cockpit brief that tells you where to spend today
CAF NOMINATIONS

Never miss a
Factory nomination

PULSE watches the Cloud Accelerate Factory data and surfaces the opportunities that are eligible but not nominated, stalled, or declined. When one qualifies, it assembles the full nomination packet — mapped to the Azure Offer Navigator form — for you to review and submit.

  • Eligible / stalled / declined nomination queue
  • Auto-resolved specialist and co-sell partner
  • Copy-ready packet aligned to Azure Offer Navigator sections
  • PULSE never submits for you — you stay in control
FY CONNECT

Your Connect,
evidenced all year

Add evidence whenever it happens — a win, a workshop, a piece of feedback. PULSE keeps it in a per-user store and, on demand, writes your Connect prose over a window you choose, mapped to the goal framework. No scramble in review season.

  • Continuous evidence capture — add items any time
  • AI-written Connect prose classified to goals 1–5
  • Generate over any lookback window you pick
  • Save snapshots you can revisit and refine
HOW IT WORKS

Lives where you already work

No desktop app. PULSE is a Microsoft 365 agent in Copilot plus a secure web dashboard, backed by your own MSX data. Here's the path a request takes:

🧑‍💻 YouTeams / M365 Copilot → 🤖 PULSE agentdrafts your report → 🛡️ Secure gatewayauth · rate-limit · audit → ⚙️ PULSE APIsAzure Functions → 🗄️ Your MSX & M365Dataverse · mail · chat · calendar → 📊 Your dashboardssecure sign-in
1

The Copilot agent

Ask PULSE to “generate my report” inside Microsoft 365 Copilot. It gathers your week and drafts it.

2

The web dashboard

Review, edit and save. Milestones, Tracker, CAF and Connect all light up with your own data.

3

Your data, secured

Per-user and keyed to your account — you sign in with your Microsoft account and PULSE only ever sees your territory.

Built to be trusted

  • The agent suggests; the dashboard writes — you approve every CRM push, comment and nomination.
  • Append-only and create-only — nothing existing is ever overwritten, closed, or duplicated.
  • Per-user isolation — your reports and Connect evidence are keyed to you alone.
  • Evidence is classified by who attended — partner-only calls never post as Direct, and never auto-push.
GET STARTED

Up and running in four steps

PULSE isn't in the company app store yet, so there's a quick one-time setup (~5 min) to add the agent. The user guide has the full walkthrough.

1

Add PULSE to Teams

Ask Daphne to add you as a collaborator, then dev.teams.microsoft.com → Apps → PULSE → Preview in Teams. No file needed. One time only.

2

Get your link code

Open the dashboard's Link page, sign in, and copy your one-time code (looks like ABCD-EFGH).

3

Link & generate

Open PULSE in Copilot, say “generate my report,” and paste your code when asked.

4

Save to your dashboard

Say “save to dashboard.” Milestones, Tracker, CAF and Connect now populate automatically.
