★ Weekly Milestones

Your week, already written.

PULSE pulls your live MSX milestones and the week's real activity — meetings, emails, follow-ups — and drafts the progress narrative for each one. You review, tweak and save; it files everything by fiscal week and can push the update back to CRM as a forecast comment, with your approval.

 

★ Edit & Push to CRM

Review, edit, push to CRM.

Every card opens into a full editor — refine the weekly progress, set the milestone status, capture next steps and add tasks, and watch the CRM comment build live in the preview. One click writes the forecast comment back to MSX and logs your tasks as activities. The text is a starting point; the final word is always yours, and only Direct customer evidence auto-pushes.

 

★ Account List

Your whole territory, mapped.

PULSE reads your deal-team assignments straight from Dataverse and lays out your full book of business — parent accounts, their child organisations, and the territory each rolls up to — de-duplicated and tagged with the SE motions in play. It's the same source of truth the Tracker and Whitespace use.

 

★ SE Execution Tracker

Every motion, scored.

A live command centre across eight strategic SE motions — SQL/Database Gravity, Fabric Attach, Compete-to-Win, Developer + Agentic, Sovereignty, Security Attach, HoK PoC and CAF. See attach coverage and the ACR sitting in the gap, scoped to your discipline — then drill into any motion to see exactly which deals have it attached and which don't, and work your territory whitespace as a prioritised, closing-soon action queue.

★ CAF Nominations

Never miss a Factory nomination.

PULSE watches the Cloud Accelerate Factory data and surfaces the opportunities that are eligible but not nominated, stalled, or declined. When one qualifies, it assembles the full nomination packet — laid out in the same sections as the Azure Offer Navigator form, with the specialist and co-sell partner auto-resolved — for you to review and submit. PULSE never submits for you.

 

★ FY Connect

Your Connect, evidenced all year.

Add evidence whenever it happens — a win, a workshop, a piece of feedback. PULSE keeps it in a per-user store and, on demand, writes your full Connect prose over a window you choose: the reflect-and-plan questions answered in your voice, and every point mapped to the goal framework with the evidence that backs it — HoK, ACR and outcomes attached. No scramble in review season.

★ Built to be trusted

The agent suggests; you approve.

Nothing touches MSX without your say-so:

✓  Append-only & create-only — nothing existing is overwritten, closed, or duplicated.
✓  Per-user isolation — your reports and evidence are keyed to you alone.
✓  Evidence is classified by who attended — partner-only calls never post as Direct, and never auto-push.
